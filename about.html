I would like to tell you some words about one of my favorite cafes in Murom. It is a suitable place to sit with a cup of tea or coffee or to spend spare time with your friends. In my humble opinion, it would be hard to find a friendlier place in Murom than this little cafe, but of course, it is a matter of taste. This coffee-house is located in Moscovskaya street near the cafe-club “Happiness”. The interior helps to create a relaxed home atmosphere. It is a small cafe, which specializes in serving coffee and desserts with a small and simple dining room.
